# IBLens Backlink Strategy

## Current State

- **Domain Age:** New (registered 2026)
- **Domain Authority:** 0 (no backlinks)
- **Indexed Pages:** ~13 (after this SEO update)
- **Competitors:** Lanterna Education, IB Pros, Revision Village, Clastify

## Timeline Expectations

- **Month 1–2:** Technical SEO indexed, long-tail keywords start appearing in Search Console
- **Month 3–4:** Resource pages begin ranking for informational queries (page 2–3)
- **Month 5–6:** With 10–20 quality backlinks, competitive pages move to page 1 for long-tail terms

## Strategy 1: IB Community Engagement (Free, High-Impact)

### Reddit
- **Subreddits:** r/IBO (180k+ members), r/6thForm, r/ApplyingToCollege
- **Approach:** Provide genuinely helpful answers to essay-related questions. Link to resource pages (NOT product pages) when directly relevant. Build karma first (2–4 weeks of pure value posts before any links).
- **Target:** 2–3 helpful comments per week with natural resource links
- **Example:** "Here's a detailed breakdown of EE criteria: [link to /resources/ib-extended-essay-guide]"

### IB Facebook Groups
- Groups like "IB Students Worldwide", "IB Extended Essay Help", "IB Class of 2027"
- Share resource articles as helpful posts, not ads
- Respond to questions with links to relevant guides

### Student Forums
- The Student Room (UK), College Confidential (US)
- Answer IB-related questions with links to guides

## Strategy 2: Guest Posting on Education Blogs (Medium Effort, High Value)

### Target Sites (DA 20–60)
1. **IB-specific blogs:** Lanterna blog, Elite IB Tutors blog, Nail IB blog
2. **General education:** The Edvocate, TeachThought, eLearning Industry
3. **Study tips sites:** Oxford Royale Academy blog, Crimson Education blog
4. **University prep:** PrepScholar blog, CollegeVine blog

### Pitch Template
Subject: Guest post — "[Topic] for IB Students"

Offer to write 1500+ word articles on topics like:
- "How AI is Changing IB Essay Preparation"
- "5 Common Extended Essay Mistakes and How to Avoid Them"
- "IB Grade Boundaries: What Students Need to Know for 2026"

Include 1–2 contextual links back to iblens.com resource pages.

### Target: 2–3 guest posts per month

## Strategy 3: Resource Link Building (Medium Effort, Sustainable)

### Create Linkable Assets
The 7 resource pages we've built are designed as linkable assets. Additional ideas:
- **IB Subject Guide Comparison Table** (which subjects pair well)
- **IB Score Calculator** (interactive tool — attracts links naturally)
- **IB Exam Schedule 2026/2027** (time-sensitive, high search volume pre-exams)

### Outreach to Resource Pages
Many education sites maintain "IB Resources" or "Study Tools" pages:
- Search: `"IB resources" intitle:resources inurl:resources`
- Search: `"useful IB links" OR "IB study tools" OR "IB revision resources"`
- Email webmasters requesting inclusion of our guides

## Strategy 4: HARO / Connectively / Featured (Low Effort, High DA)

- Sign up for journalist query services
- Respond to education-related queries as "IB education technology expert"
- Topics: AI in education, exam preparation, international education trends
- Even 1–2 mentions in high-DA publications (DA 60+) significantly moves the needle

## Strategy 5: YouTube & Social Proof

### YouTube
- Create short (3–5 min) videos: "How to Structure Your IB Extended Essay" with link in description
- YouTube descriptions with links count as backlinks and drive referral traffic
- Target: 1 video per week initially

### TikTok / Instagram Reels
- Short-form content about IB tips drives brand searches
- Brand searches → Google sees demand → improves rankings

---

## Strategy 6: Partnerships with IB Tutors

- Many IB tutors have personal websites or blogs
- Offer free IBLens credits in exchange for a review/mention on their site
- Target: 5–10 tutor partnerships in first 3 months

## Metrics to Track

- **Google Search Console:** Impressions, clicks, average position per page
- **Ahrefs/Moz:** Domain Rating, referring domains, backlink count
- **Target:** 20 referring domains within 3 months, 50 within 6 months
- **Key pages to monitor:** Homepage, /essay, /resources/ib-extended-essay-guide, /resources/ib-grade-boundaries

---

## What NOT to Do

- Do NOT buy links from PBNs or link farms
- Do NOT spam forums with promotional links
- Do NOT use automated link-building tools
- Do NOT create low-quality guest posts just for links
- Do NOT exchange links reciprocally (Google detects this)

All links should be editorially placed and provide genuine value to the reader.
